<p>If you're leading digital commerce, omnichannel marketing, or retail media for a global CPG brand, you're navigating a perfect storm of complexity. Your consumers expect seamless shopping experiences across countless touchpoints. Your finance team wants clean ROI measurement. And your marketing technology stack keeps expanding with yet another point solution for each new channel.</p> 
<p>The result? Fragmented shopper journeys, operational headaches, and revenue left on the table.</p> 
<p>The most forward-thinking brands aren't just adding more tools—they're fundamentally rethinking their approach. They're building what we call the future-proof martech stack, anchored by a strategic centralized commerce layer. This isn't just another buzzword; it's the essential architecture that transforms scattered commerce initiatives into a cohesive growth engine.</p>
